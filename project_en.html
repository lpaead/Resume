Maritime Safety AI Monitoring System
The Maritime Safety AI Monitoring System is a control and web service designed to minimize human casualties at beaches through features such as rip current detection, beach boundary monitoring, and personnel identification. This system leverages cutting-edge AI technology to enable prompt and accurate responses in coordination with the coast guard.
